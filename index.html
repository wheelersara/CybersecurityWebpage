Malware

There are many threats to computers

Malware is one of the biggest threats to software, data security, and the integrity of a device.

Malware can completely destroy a system and result in data loss.

There are 6 major known types of malware

  • Virus
  • Worms
  • Trojan Virus
  • Spyware
  • Adware
  • Ransomware

Phishing

We must understand how not everything that we see online is accurate and trustworthy

Phishing is a method of social engineering that attempts to get a user to visit a malicious website or give out personal information.

Phishing is designed to take advantage of people and steal information.

DDoS Attacks

DDoS attacks are direct attacks to a server or network.

These attacks send heavy traffic to a server to essentially overwhelm it and freeze the system.

It essentially makes a machine or server useless and prevents any activity to occur.
