Phishing Prevention

Phishing can be minimized in organizations by the employer providing training on phishing, or by testing users not to click on suspicious emails and verifying the sender of emails. KnowBe4 is a reputable software that can be used to do this. Users can also report the suspicious emails to be investigated by clicking a "phish alert" button.
